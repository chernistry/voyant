Travel Assistant Performance Dashboard

Monitor key performance indicators for the Voyant travel assistant. These metrics provide insights into user satisfaction, system reliability, and operational efficiency.

Dashboard Guide

Key metrics and their business significance:

First Call Resolution (FCR) Measures the percentage of customer issues resolved in the first interaction. Target: >60%
Citation Coverage & Verification Ensures answers are grounded in reliable sources. High coverage and low failure rates indicate trustworthy responses.
Router Confidence Low confidence scores indicate ambiguous user intents that may require clarification or retraining.
External Request Latency Measures the performance of external APIs. High latency impacts user experience and should be monitored closely.
End-to-End Latency Overall response time. Critical for user satisfaction. Target: <3s for 95th percentile.
